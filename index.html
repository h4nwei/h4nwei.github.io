About Me

I am currently a Research Scientist in the Alibaba-NTU Global e-Sustainability CorpLab (ANGEL) at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), working with Prof. Weisi Lin. My current research interests focus on green and energy-efficient AI technologies that prioritize sustainable ecosystems and lifestyles.

My general research interests lie in:

  • Computer Vision: Image Quality Assessment, Video Quality Assessment
  • Computational Photography: Low-light Image Enhancement, High Dynamic Range Imaging
  • Generative Model: Large Multimodal Model, Diffusion Model
  • Green AI: Coreset Selection, Model Compression

I am always looking for opportunities to collaborate with researchers in related fields. Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research.

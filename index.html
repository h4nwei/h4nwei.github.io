About Me

I am currently a final-year Ph.D student advised by Prof. Shiqi Wang. Prior to studying at CityU, I received my B.E and M.S. degrees from the Jiangxi University of Finance and Economics, under the supervision of Prof. Yuming Fang and Prof. Kede Ma.

My interests are centered around Computer Vision and Deep Learning. Current research topics include:

  • Computer Vision: Perceptual Visual Quality Assessment, VR Scanpath Prediction.
  • Computational Photography: High Dynamic Range Imaging, Low-light Image Enhancement, Multi-Exposure Image Fusion.
  • Generative Models: Large Multimodal Model, Diffusion Model
